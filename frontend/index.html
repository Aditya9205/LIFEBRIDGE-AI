🩹 Interactive Rapid First-Aid Lookup

❤️

CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation)

  1. Place hands centered on the chest.
  2. Push hard and fast: **100–120 compressions per minute** at a depth of 2 inches.
  3. Allow chest to rise fully between compressions.
  4. Provide **30 compressions followed by 2 rescue breaths** if trained. Continue until help arrives.
🩸

Severe Bleeding Control

  1. Apply firm, direct pressure on the wound with a clean cloth or sterile dressing.
  2. Maintain continuous pressure; do not remove dressing to look. Add layers if soaked.
  3. Elevate the bleeding limb above heart level if possible.
  4. If bleeding is catastrophic and uncontrollable on a limb, apply a **tourniquet** high and tight.
☀️

Heat Stroke Emergency

  1. Move the casualty immediately to a cool, shaded environment.
  2. Cool the body rapidly: apply wet towels, spray water, and fan the individual.
  3. Place cold packs on neck, armpits, and groin.
  4. Do **not** give fluids if they are unconscious or vomiting. Seek medical attention immediately.
🔥

Thermal & Chemical Burns

  1. Cool the burn under cool running water for at least **10 minutes**. Do not use ice.
  2. Remove rings or jewelry from the burned area before it starts swelling.
  3. Cover the burn loosely with sterile non-stick bandage or clean plastic wrap.
  4. Do **not** pop blisters or apply oil, butter, or toothpaste.
